Approach

A loop, not a line.

Discovery is not a phase you finish. We run the loop, discover, understand, validate, build, learn, until the evidence is clear and the right decision is the obvious one.

Field notes

Where founders usually start.

LOG 01

"We keep shipping features. Adoption stays flat. Every launch feels like a coin flip."

What a discovery sprint changes

The roadmap stops being a list of guesses. You know which problem is real, who has it, and why past launches missed.

LOG 02

"We have traction but three plausible directions. The team is split and every week costs us focus."

What a strategy sprint changes

One direction, chosen on evidence instead of the loudest voice. A decision the team owns and will not relitigate.

LOG 03

"Everyone says we should do something with AI. Nobody can say what, or why it would matter to our customers."

What an AI sprint changes

A short list of use cases ranked by real value and feasibility, and a first experiment scoped small enough to start this month.
